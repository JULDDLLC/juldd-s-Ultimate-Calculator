Tipping Guide

Tipping customs vary by country and service quality:

  • 15-20%: Standard for good service in the US
  • 10-15%: Acceptable for average service
  • 20-25%: For exceptional service
  • 0-10%: Many countries outside the US have different tipping expectations

Remember: In some countries, service charges may already be included in the bill.
